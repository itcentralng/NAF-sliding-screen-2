Lieutenant Colonel FO Fajobi (NAF/281)

Chief Paymaster — 1 Sep 71 to 14 Nov 75

As the first leader of the Nigerian Air Force Finance Specialty, Lieutenant Colonel FO Folaji laid the bedrock of integrity and innovation during Nigeria’s formative post-civil war era. He pioneered the foundational systems that ensured timely remuneration for Nigeria’s earliest air defenders, navigating logistical challenges and limited institutional establishment. His visionary initiatives of standardized pay protocols and ethical procurement practices became the blueprint for generations. He personally championed welfare advances for personnel, ensuring no airman’s family endured hardship. Lieutenant Colonel Folaji’s indelible mark transcends time, a testament to the saying that ‘the boldness of pioneers defines the destiny of institutions’.

WING COMMANDER LA DANGAJI (NAF/274)

Acting Director of Finance and Account — 14 Nov 75 to 11 Apr 79

Wing Commander LA Dangaji transformed financial governance through budget precision and innovative resource optimization frameworks. He pioneering integration of accounting principles into NAF finances and revolutionized forecasting accuracy, ensuring seamless support for high-stakes missions. Beyond numbers, he fostered collaboration between various NAF departments that bridged gaps between policy and execution. His legacy of a young Finance Specialty empowered by agility, transparency, and unshakable trust stands as a testament to leadership that turns vision into victory.
